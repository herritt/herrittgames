Herritt Games - Barnes Early Build

I'm creating a simulator of Barnes House - which was the residence I stayed at in the 90s.

Click the view to lock your cursor so you can look around correctly. Use arrow keys or W A S D keys to move your character. Once you've clicked the view, you won't be able to see your cursor - click ESC to see your cursor again.

Current Features

    You can walk around with the mouse and WASD keys, W is forward in the direction of the mouse, A is left, S is down, and D is back.
    Press SPACE to jump
    2022.01.08 - Added a wall and duplicated the room wood furniture model to start building a template room.
    2022.01.09 - Added walls around a room, a door opening and door jam. Duplicated the room prefab to show two rooms.
